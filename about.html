About us

Let Us Tell You Our Story

AKASH ENTERPRISES is situated in INDORE (MP) India, established in the Year 1998 . We are reputed and well known Manufacturer & Exporter-Importer of Kabuli Chickpeas, Black Chickpeas,Soybean and Wheat and have established ourselves successfully in Indian market as well as world market. We believe to keep 100% transparency in all our business dealings with our valuable customers.

We have established ourselves as a recognized and trustable Agro product Suppliers. We are registered with Agricultural and Processed Food Products Export Development Authority (APEDA ,recognized and approved by Government of India).

Company Mission

To give excellent services to our customerswhere delight & satisfaction is our prime focus.We believe that this is the key to support a long term of vendor and business relationship. We are growing day by day in global and domestic market and itis made possible with our well trusted network of suppliers spread across the country.We strive to expand our selves globally by providing quality goods and services to our customers and developing a strong long term business relationship with them .

Company's strength

With the expansion and continuous development of the company we have a strong support of farmers and producers across different states in India with a systematic transportation system across different states of India for procurements of best quality of Agro products and achieve timely shipping schedule.
